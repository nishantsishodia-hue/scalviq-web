Mastering The Ecosystem

Every platform has a unique algorithm and strict specific requirements. Treating them all the same is a guaranteed path to failure. Here is what it takes to win on each.

🛒
Amazon India
Conquering the A9 Algorithm
What Is Required
  • Brand Registry: Essential for protecting your listings and unlocking A+ Content (EBC).
  • Prime Eligibility: Products must be in FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon) to win trust and fast delivery badges.
  • Pure White Imaging: Strict adherence to RGB 255,255,255 backgrounds for main images.
Our Strategy
  • Backend Keyword Mapping: We inject hidden search terms into the backend to rank for queries your competitors miss.
  • Buy-Box Dominance: Dynamic pricing strategies and review management to ensure you never lose the buy button.
  • AMS Optimization: Structuring Sponsored Products and Brand ads to lower ACOS and protect profit margins.
🛍️
Flipkart
Visibility & Tier Optimization
What Is Required
  • FBF (Flipkart Assured): Mandatory compliance to get the trusted badge and visibility boost.
  • Tier Progression: Moving from Bronze to Gold/Diamond tiers requires strict SLA and low return rates.
  • Pricing Psychology: Structuring MRP and selling price to align with Flipkart's heavy-discount audience behavior.
Our Strategy
  • PLA (Product Listing Ads): Precision bidding on PLA to capture top-of-the-page visibility during sale events.
  • Event Participation: Strategic inventory planning for Big Billion Days and category-specific sales.
  • Regional Search Focus: Optimizing titles based on tier-2 and tier-3 city search patterns in India.
👗
Myntra
Premium Fashion & Lifestyle
What Is Required
  • Brand Trademark (TM): Myntra only onboards registered, legitimate brands with a strong identity.
  • Premium Cataloging: High-end, strict-guideline photoshoot imagery. Standard flat-lays get rejected immediately.
  • Minimum SKU Count: A specific range of styles to qualify for their onboarding review process.
Our Strategy
  • Onboarding & Approval: We handle the rigorous documentation and cataloging required to get your brand approved.
  • Myntra Ads (PLA/Brand): Strategic ad placements to build initial traction among high-paying demographics.
  • EORS Optimization: Inventory and pricing strategies specifically tailored for Myntra's 'End of Reason Sale'.
