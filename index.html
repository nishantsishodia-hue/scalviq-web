How does the AI Catalog Photoshoot work?
+
Share clear product photos via WhatsApp or email — or courier your physical products to our Gurugram studio. Our AI processes every image with professional backgrounds, precision lighting correction, shadow removal, and platform-specific formatting for Amazon, Flipkart, Instagram, and your website. Finished images are delivered via Google Drive within 24 hours. No studio rental, no photographer fees. Contact us for affordable pricing based on your product count and category.
Why is pricing not listed on the website?
+
Because every business is genuinely different. A local restaurant has different needs and a different budget than a national e-commerce brand. We customise pricing based on your exact scope, goals, and timeline — so you always receive the best possible rate for precisely what your business needs, with nothing wasted. We guarantee the most competitive pricing in the market with zero hidden charges. Contact us and we will share a clear, no-obligation quote within 2 hours.
What experience does the SCALVIQ team have in political campaigns?
+
Our team has direct, hands-on experience working inside a political leader's office — managing their digital strategy, voter outreach communications, media coordination, event management, and public narrative. This is not academic knowledge — it is real-world experience from inside an actual political operation. That understanding of how political communication works at ground level is what makes our political campaigns genuinely effective.
Can SCALVIQ serve clients outside Gurugram?
+
Absolutely. We are headquartered in Gurugram but actively serve clients across India — Delhi NCR, Haryana, Rajasthan, Punjab, Uttarakhand, Mumbai, and beyond. Our workflow is fully digital — strategy sessions on Google Meet, content and reports delivered via Google Drive, and daily updates on WhatsApp. For AI catalog photoshoots, simply courier your products to us or share high-resolution photographs directly.
How quickly will I see results from digital marketing?
+
Social media management shows measurable growth within 30–45 days. Paid advertising campaigns on Meta and Google typically show meaningful results within 7–14 days of launch. SEO delivers significant organic traction in 60–90 days. We are fully transparent about realistic timelines, provide detailed monthly performance reports, and never overpromise. Our reputation is built on consistent, honest over-delivery — not on unrealistic guarantees.
What is the payment structure?
+
We require 50% advance payment before commencing any project. The remaining 50% is due at month-end for ongoing retainers, or on delivery milestone for project-based work. We accept UPI, NEFT, and all major payment methods. No payment of any kind is requested before we have agreed on the full scope, timeline, and pricing together in writing. Your money is always protected.
