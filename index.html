How does the AI Catalog Photoshoot work?
+
Share clear product photos or courier physical items to our Gurugram studio. Our AI pipeline enhances images — professional backgrounds, lighting correction, shadow removal, and platform-specific formatting. Finished images arrive within 24 hours via Google Drive. No studio rental, no photographer fees. Contact us for pricing based on your product volume and category.
Why don't you show prices on the website?
+
Because no two businesses are the same. A neighbourhood shop and a national brand have entirely different needs. We customise pricing so you receive the best possible rate for your exact scope — not an overpriced package with services you do not need. Contact us and our team will provide a transparent, competitive quote.
What experience does the SCALVIQ team have in political campaigns?
+
Our team has worked directly within a political office — managing digital communication, voter outreach, public narrative strategy, and constituency-level campaign coordination in Haryana and Delhi. This is not theoretical knowledge — it is direct, inside experience of how winning campaigns are built and executed on the ground and online.
We are based outside Gurugram. Can SCALVIQ still help us?
+
Absolutely. We are headquartered in Gurugram but work with clients across India — Delhi NCR, Haryana, Rajasthan, Mumbai, and beyond. Our workflow is fully digital: strategy sessions on Google Meet, content via Google Drive, and updates via WhatsApp. For AI catalog photoshoots, simply courier products to us or share high-resolution photographs.
What is the typical timeline to see results?
+
Social media management produces visible growth within 30–45 days. Paid advertising campaigns show measurable results within 7–14 days of launch. SEO delivers meaningful organic traction over 60–90 days. We provide full transparency on timelines from the outset, with detailed monthly reports tracking every key metric.
What are your payment terms?

A 50% advance is required before work commences on any project. The remaining balance is due at month-end for ongoing retainers, or upon delivery for one-time projects.
