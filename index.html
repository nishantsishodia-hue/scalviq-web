How does the AI Catalog Photoshoot work?
+
Share clear product photos or courier physical products to our Gurugram studio. Our AI enhances images with professional backgrounds, lighting correction, shadow removal, and platform-specific formatting. Finished images are delivered within 24 hours via Google Drive. No studio cost, no photographer charges. Contact us for the most affordable pricing based on your product count and category.
Why is pricing not shown on the website?
+
Because every business is genuinely different. A local shop has different requirements than a national e-commerce brand. We customise pricing based on your exact scope and goals — so you always get the most affordable rate for precisely what your business needs. Contact us and we guarantee you the best price in the market with zero hidden charges.
What is your team's experience in political campaigns?
+
Our leadership team includes someone who worked directly inside a political office as PA to a senior leader — managing digital presence, communication strategy, voter connect programs, and media coordination. This real inside experience is what makes SCALVIQ Politics genuinely effective. We have lived and worked in political environments — we don't just theorise about them.
Can SCALVIQ work with clients outside Gurugram?
+
Absolutely. We are headquartered in Gurugram but serve clients across India — Delhi NCR, Haryana, Rajasthan, Punjab, Mumbai, and beyond. Our entire workflow is digital — strategy calls on Google Meet, content delivery via Google Drive, and regular updates on WhatsApp. For AI catalog photoshoots, simply courier products or share high-resolution photographs.
How quickly should I expect to see results?
+
Social media management shows measurable growth in 30–45 days. Paid advertising campaigns (Meta and Google) produce results within 7–14 days of launch. SEO delivers meaningful organic traction in 60–90 days. We are completely transparent about timelines, send detailed monthly reports, and never overpromise. Our reputation is built on consistent over-delivery, not unrealistic guarantees.
What is the payment structure?
+
We require 50% advance payment before commencing any project. The remaining 50% is due at month-end for ongoing retainers, or on delivery for project-based work. We accept UPI, NEFT, and all major payment methods. No payment is ever requested before we agree on scope, timeline, and pricing together in writing.
