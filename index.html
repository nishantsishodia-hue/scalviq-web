FAQ
Questions we
always get asked.
How does the AI Catalog Photoshoot work?
+
You share clear product photos (or courier physical products to our Gurugram studio). Our AI enhances the images — professional backgrounds, lighting correction, shadow removal, and platform-specific formatting. You receive finished images within 24 hours via Google Drive. No studio rental, no photographer charges. Contact us to get the best affordable pricing for your product count.
Why don't you show pricing on the website?
+
Because every business is different. A small local shop has different needs than a large e-commerce brand. We customise our pricing based on your specific requirements, scope, and goals. This way you always get the most affordable rate for exactly what you need — not a fixed package that doesn't fit. Contact us and we promise to give you the best price in the market.
What is your payment structure?
+
We require 50% advance payment before starting any project. The remaining 50% is due at the end of the month for ongoing services. For one-time projects, we work on a milestone basis — 50% upfront, 50% on delivery. We accept UPI, bank transfer, and all major payment methods. No hidden charges ever.
Do you work with political parties and election candidates?
+
Yes — SCALVIQ Politics is one of our strongest verticals. We work with individual candidates, political parties, and campaign managers for all levels — panchayat, municipal, MLA, MP elections. We provide end-to-end digital campaign management including strategy, content, ads, voter connect programs, and live event coverage across Haryana, Delhi NCR, and pan-India.
I am outside Gurugram — can you still work with me?
+
Absolutely. We are based in Gurugram but serve clients across India — Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Jaipur, Chandigarh, Lucknow, and beyond. Our entire workflow is digital — strategy calls on Google Meet, content delivery via Google Drive, and reporting via email and WhatsApp. For AI catalog photoshoots, you can courier products to us or share high-quality photos.
How quickly will I see results from digital marketing?
+
Social media management shows visible growth in 30–45 days. Paid ads (Meta and Google) show results within 7–14 days of campaign launch. SEO takes 60–90 days for meaningful organic results. We are completely transparent about timelines and never promise overnight miracles — but we do promise consistent, measurable improvement every single month with detailed reports.
