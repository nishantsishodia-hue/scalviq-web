How does the AI Catalog Photoshoot work?
+
Share clear product photos via WhatsApp or email — or courier products to our Gurugram studio. Our AI processes every image: professional backgrounds, lighting correction, shadow removal, and platform-specific formatting. Finished images delivered via Google Drive within 24 hours. Contact us for pricing based on your product count.
Why is pricing not shown on the website?
+
Every business is different. We customise pricing based on your exact scope and goals — so you always get the most affordable rate for precisely what you need. We guarantee the most competitive pricing with zero hidden charges. Contact us for a clear quote within 2 hours.
What political campaign experience does the team have?
+
Our team has direct hands-on experience working inside a political leader's office — managing digital strategy, voter outreach, media coordination, event management, and public narrative. Real-world experience from inside an actual political operation. That inside understanding is what makes our work genuinely effective.
Can SCALVIQ serve clients outside Gurugram?
+
Absolutely. We serve clients across India — Delhi NCR, Haryana, Rajasthan, Punjab, Mumbai, and beyond. Our workflow is fully digital: strategy calls on Google Meet, content via Google Drive, updates on WhatsApp. For AI catalog photoshoots, courier products or share high-resolution photos.
How quickly will I see results?
+
Social media: 30–45 days. Paid ads (Meta/Google): 7–14 days from launch. SEO: 60–90 days for meaningful organic traction. We are fully transparent about timelines, send detailed monthly reports, and never overpromise. Our reputation is built on consistent over-delivery.
What is the payment structure?
+
50% advance before commencing. Remaining 50% at month-end for retainers, or on delivery for project work. We accept UPI, NEFT, and all major payment methods. No payment requested before we agree on scope, timeline, and pricing in writing.
