How We Execute Your Campaign

Humne actual politicians ke office ke andar baithkar elections manage kiye hain. Humari approach theory nahi, on-ground reality par based hai.

01
Data & Intelligence

Constituency & Voter Mapping

Sabse pehle humari team aapki constituency ka data organize karti hai. Hum identify karte hain ki kaunsi caste, religion aur umar (age) ke voters kis booth par hain. Is data ke bina andhere me teer chalane jaisa hai. Hum voter behavior aur past voting trends ka deeply analysis karte hain.

02
Perception Management

Image Building & Core Narrative

Public me aapki chhavi (image) kya hai? Hum leader ke brand ko ek strong, decisive aur relatable narrative me badalte hain. Premium video production, solid speeches aur emotional connect wali copywriting se hum aapke paksh me "Hawa" (Wave) banate hain.

03
Execution

Booth-Level WhatsApp Connect

Sarkari mass-SMS kaam nahi karte. Hum aapke karyakartao (workers) ke data ko organize karke hyper-local WhatsApp groups banate hain. Har booth ke voters ko unke muddo (issues) aur unki bhasha me daily targeted messages bhejte hain, taaki voting day par turnout maximum ho.

04
Defense & Offense

24/7 Digital War Room

Election me har ghanta zaroori hota hai. Humara War Room 24/7 active rehta hai. Opponent ki har chaal, rally, aur social media post ko monitor karke rapid response aur counter-attacks design kiye jaate hain, taaki opposition ko aapke narrative pe haavi hone ka mauka na mile.

Core Campaign Services

Hum aapke digital campaign ka A to Z handle karte hain. Aap ground pe miliye, digital ground hum sambhalenge.

📱

Social Media Handling

Aapke Facebook, Twitter (X), aur Instagram accounts ka poora control. Daily updates, comments ka reply, aur negative comments ko hide karna taaki ek active aur mazboot leader ki chhavi bani rahe.

🎬

Post & Reel Creation

Boring text nahi. Hum aapki speeches, ground visits aur muddo par high-quality graphics aur viral Reels banate hain. Aisa content jo janta khud apne dosto ke sath share karna pasand kare.

💬

WhatsApp Management

Booth-level WhatsApp groups banana aur daily targeted messages bhejna. Direct voter connect bina kisi spam ke, unhi ki local bhasha aur muddo (issues) ke sath.

📰

PR & Local Media

Local news portals, YouTube channels, aur digital akhbaro par aapke positive interviews, press releases, aur articles publish karwana taaki perception aapke favor me set rahe.

Crisis Management & Action

Elections me problems aani tay hain. Dekhiye ground situations me SCALVIQ kaise react karta hai.

🚨 Situation 01
Opponent ne achanak election se theek pehle fake news ya negative PR viral kar di jisse voter base hil sakta hai.
⚡ SCALVIQ Action
Humara War Room turant alert hoga. 1 ghante ke andar fact-check video aur strong counter-narrative banega. Hum use apne network ke zariye booth-level WhatsApp groups aur Facebook par aggressively push karenge, taaki janta tak sachai damage hone se pehle pahunch jaye.
🚨 Situation 02
Constituency me ek specific caste ya community ka bada vote bank hai, par abhi tak wo undecided hain (kis party ko vote denge).
⚡ SCALVIQ Action
Hum voter mapping data nikalenge. Phir sirf us area aur un logon ko target karke unke core muddo (issues) par targeted Meta/Google ads chalayenge. Unhe wahi message dikhega jo unke samaj me relatable ho, jisse unka jhukaav aapki taraf badhega.
🚨 Situation 03
Ground level karyakarta (party workers) active nahi hain, unka data unorganized hai, aur pata nahi kaun kahan kaam kar raha hai.
⚡ SCALVIQ Action
Hum aapke karyakartao ke liye ek custom tech tool (simple mobile app/dashboard) banayenge. Isme har worker ki attendance aur ground activity track hogi. Unhe daily digital tasks diye jayenge, jisse poori party force discipline me ek machinery ki tarah kaam karegi.
